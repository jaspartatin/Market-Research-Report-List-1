<p><p>Electrical insulating mats are categorized based on voltage levels into low voltage, medium voltage, and high voltage types. Low voltage mats are designed for use in environments up to 1 kV, providing safety against electric shocks in residential or commercial settings. Medium voltage mats cater to voltages between 1 kV and 36 kV, typically used in industrial applications. High voltage mats are intended for voltages over 36 kV, offering maximum protection in power generation and transmission facilities. Each type ensures safety and reduces the risk of electrical accidents.</p></p>
