<p><p>The medical tubing market consists of various types, each tailored for specific applications. PVC (polyvinyl chloride) tubing is widely used due to its versatility and cost-effectiveness. Polyolefin tubing offers excellent chemical resistance and flexibility. TPE (thermoplastic elastomer) and TPU (thermoplastic polyurethane) are prized for their elasticity and durability. Silicone tubing is favored for its biocompatibility and temperature resilience. Other materials may include rubber and fluoropolymers, each serving niche applications in medical environments, including fluid delivery and patient monitoring.</p></p>
