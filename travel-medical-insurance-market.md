<p><ul><li>Stand-alone Medical Insurance</li><li>As Part of Comprehensive Travel Insurance</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The travel medical insurance market primarily consists of two types: stand-alone medical insurance and comprehensive travel insurance. Stand-alone medical insurance provides coverage specifically for medical emergencies during travel, ensuring that travelers have access to healthcare services abroad without affecting their primary health insurance. In contrast, comprehensive travel insurance incorporates medical coverage along with other travel-related protections, such as trip cancellations, lost luggage, and travel delays, offering travelers a more holistic safety net during their journeys.</p></p>
