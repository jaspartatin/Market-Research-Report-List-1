<p><p>C-Band satellite transponders operate across three frequency ranges: high frequency (HF), intermediate frequency (IF), and low frequency (LF). High frequency transponders, typically between 5.850 to 6.425 GHz, are used for long-distance communication and data transmission. Intermediate frequency transponders, ranging from 4.500 to 4.800 GHz, offer a balance of range and signal integrity for regional coverage. Low frequency transponders, below 4.500 GHz, provide greater penetration through obstacles, making them suitable for rural and remote areas with limited infrastructure.</p></p>
