<p><p>The flammability test chamber market consists of two primary types: horizontal flammability chambers and vertical flammability chambers. Horizontal chambers are designed for testing materials in a flat orientation, allowing for assessments of surface burning characteristics. Conversely, vertical chambers assess how materials behave when subjected to flame in an upright position, simulating real-world fire scenarios. Both types ensure compliance with safety standards and are crucial for industries like textiles, construction, and automotive in evaluating material fire resistance.</p></p>
