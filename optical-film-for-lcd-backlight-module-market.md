<p><p>The Optical Film for LCD Backlight Module market includes three main types: Reflective Film, Diffusion Membrane, and Light Guide Membrane. Reflective Film enhances light efficiency by reflecting unused light back into the display. Diffusion Membrane spreads light evenly across the screen, improving uniformity and color consistency. Light Guide Membrane directs light from backlights to specific areas of the display, optimizing brightness and visibility. Together, these films are essential for enhancing the performance and quality of LCD screens.</p></p>
