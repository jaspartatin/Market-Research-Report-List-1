<p><p>The Mud Tire (MT) market primarily features two types: radial tires and bias-ply tires. Radial tires utilize flexible sidewalls and an angled tread, enhancing traction and durability on off-road terrain while providing better road handling. In contrast, bias-ply tires have a stiffer construction with overlapping plies, offering a more rugged design ideal for extreme conditions. Each type caters to different user preferences and driving conditions, influencing performance, comfort, and load capacity for off-road and mud applications.</p></p>
