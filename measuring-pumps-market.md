<p><p>The measuring pumps market can be categorized into two main types: fixed displacement measuring pumps and variable displacement measuring pumps. Fixed displacement pumps deliver a consistent volume of fluid per cycle, making them ideal for applications requiring precise metering. In contrast, variable displacement pumps can adjust their output volume based on system demands, offering flexibility in varying operational conditions. Both types serve essential roles in industries such as chemical processing, water treatment, and food manufacturing, ensuring accurate fluid handling.</p></p>
