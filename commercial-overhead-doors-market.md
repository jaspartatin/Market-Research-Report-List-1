<p><p>The commercial overhead doors market comprises several types, including sectional doors, rolling doors, and high-speed doors. Rolling doors consist of slats that roll up into a coil, providing durability and space-saving benefits, suitable for retail and industrial environments. High-speed doors operate at rapid speeds, enhancing traffic flow and energy efficiency, making them ideal for environments requiring frequent access, such as food processing and logistics facilities.</p></p>
