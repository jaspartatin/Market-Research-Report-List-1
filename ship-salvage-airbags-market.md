High Bearing Airbags are designed for more significant weight support, suitable for medium-sized ships and larger salvage tasks. Super-bearing Airbags, the most robust option, can handle extremely heavy loads and are ideal for large marine salvage operations. Each type is engineered for specific buoyancy and lifting capabilities in ship recovery processes.</p></p>
