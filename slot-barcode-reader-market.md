<p><p>The Slot Barcode Reader market is divided into two main types based on the wavelength of light used: Visible Light and Infrared. Visible Light Barcode Readers utilize standard light to scan and decode barcodes, making them effective in typical lighting conditions. In contrast, Infrared Barcode Readers employ infrared light, which can enhance reading capabilities in low-light environments and improve durability against extreme conditions. Both types cater to diverse applications across retail, logistics, and inventory management sectors.</p></p>
