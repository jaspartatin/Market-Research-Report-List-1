<p><strong>The Contact Smart Cards Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active card</li><li>Passive card</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Contact smart cards are categorized into active and passive types based on their power sources and functionality. Active cards contain a built-in battery, enabling them to power advanced features like biometric authentication and wireless communication. They typically offer enhanced processing capabilities and storage. In contrast, passive cards do not have a battery; they draw power from the card reader when in proximity. While simpler in function, passive cards are widely used for applications like access control, payments, and identification due to their cost-effectiveness.</p></p>
