<p><strong>The Cylinder Liner Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Dry Cylinder Liner</li><li>Wet Cylinder Liner</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cylinder liner market is divided into dry and wet types, each serving distinct purposes in engine design. Dry cylinder liners are installed without direct coolant contact, relying instead on the engine block for heat dissipation, making them ideal for high-performance applications. In contrast, wet cylinder liners are immersed in the coolant, allowing for efficient heat removal and improved engine thermal management, making them suitable for heavy-duty vehicles. The choice between them depends on factors like engine type and operational requirements.</p></p>
