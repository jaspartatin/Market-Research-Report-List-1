<p><p>The spinal cord trauma treatment market is classified into two main types: complete spinal cord injuries and partial spinal cord injuries. Complete spinal cord injuries result in total loss of motor and sensory function below the injury site, necessitating extensive rehabilitation and adaptive technologies. In contrast, partial spinal cord injuries allow for some retained function, focusing on therapies to enhance recovery and improve quality of life. Both segments require specialized interventions tailored to the severity and unique challenges of each injury type.</p></p>
