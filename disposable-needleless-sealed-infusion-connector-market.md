<p><p>The disposable needleless sealed infusion connector market features three main types: positive pressure connectors, which maintain pressure to prevent blood reflux during disconnections; negative pressure connectors, designed to use a vacuum effect to prevent fluid backflow; and balanced pressure connectors, which balance pressure in and out to minimize risk of embolism and maintain fluid flow. Each type aims to improve safety and efficiency in intravenous therapy by reducing infection risks and enhancing ease of use for healthcare providers.</p></p>
