<p><p>The LED driving market features two main types of power supplies: external and built-in. External power supplies are separate units that provide the necessary voltage and current for LED operation, offering flexibility in design and installation. They are often used in applications requiring higher power levels. In contrast, built-in power supplies are integrated directly within the LED fixture, allowing for more compact designs and streamlined installations. Both types cater to various applications, depending on space, efficiency, and power requirements.</p></p>
