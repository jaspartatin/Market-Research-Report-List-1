<p><p>Raiseboring equipment encompasses various drilling technologies used for underground operations. Impact drills utilize high-speed rotating bits to create large diameter holes, ideal for vertical raises. Rotary drills employ a rotating drill string with drill bits, providing efficient and precise drilling for both vertical and horizontal applications. Vibratory drills utilize vibration to enhance penetration and reduce soil resistance, making them effective for challenging terrains.
