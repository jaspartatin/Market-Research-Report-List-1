<p><p>The makeup brushes market is primarily divided into two types: natural hair brushes and synthetic hair brushes. Natural hair brushes are made from animal hair, such as squirrel or goat, offering superior pigment pick-up and a soft application, making them favored by many professional makeup artists. In contrast, synthetic hair brushes are crafted from materials like nylon or polyester, providing greater durability, easier cleaning, and cruelty-free options, making them increasingly popular among environmentally conscious consumers and those with sensitive skin.</p></p>
