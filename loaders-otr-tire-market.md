<p><p>The Loaders OTR (Off-The-Road) Tire market is primarily segmented into two types: radial tires and bias tires. Radial tires feature layers that run perpendicular to the direction of travel, providing improved traction, stability, and longer lifespan, making them ideal for heavy-duty applications. In contrast, bias tires have layers that crisscross, offering a stiffer sidewall and superior load-carrying capability, which is beneficial for certain terrains. Both types cater to diverse operational needs in construction, mining, and agricultural sectors.</p></p>
