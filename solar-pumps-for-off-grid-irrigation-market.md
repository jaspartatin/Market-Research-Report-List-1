<p><p>Solar pumps for off-grid irrigation primarily fall into two categories: submersible pumps and surface pumps. Submersible pumps are designed to be submerged in water sources, effectively lifting water from deep wells or aquifers. They are ideal for areas with fluctuating water levels. In contrast, surface pumps draw water from above ground sources, such as rivers or tanks. Both types utilize solar energy, making them sustainable solutions for irrigation in remote areas, reducing reliance on fossil fuels and enhancing agricultural productivity.</p></p>
