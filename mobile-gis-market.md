<p><p>The Mobile GIS market can be categorized into two main types: On-Premise and On-Cloud. On-Premise Mobile GIS involves software that is installed on local servers, providing organizations with full control over their data and infrastructure. This option is often preferred for security-sensitive applications. Conversely, On-Cloud Mobile GIS leverages cloud computing, allowing users to access GIS services through the internet. This model offers scalability, lower initial costs, and ease of access from various devices, making it popular for dynamic operations.</p></p>
