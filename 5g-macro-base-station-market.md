<p><ul><li>BBU</li><li>AAU</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The 5G macro base station market consists of two main components: Baseband Units (BBUs) and Active Antenna Units (AAUs). BBUs manage data processing and communication, handling traffic between the core network and the radio units. AAUs integrate antennas and radio functions into a single unit, enhancing signal quality and performance. Together, they enable efficient coverage and capacity for 5G networks, supporting diverse applications. This market is vital for telecom operators seeking to implement robust and high-speed mobile communication infrastructure.</p></p>
