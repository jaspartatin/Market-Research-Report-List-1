<p><p>Bone Viscosupplementation is a medical treatment primarily aimed at alleviating pain and improving joint function in conditions such as osteoarthritis. The procedure involves the injection of viscoelastic substances, typically hyaluronic acid, directly into the affected joint. These substances mimic the natural synovial fluid, enhancing lubrication and reducing friction during movement, which can lead to improved mobility and decreased discomfort.</p><p>The Bone Viscosupplementation Market is experiencing significant growth driven by an increasing prevalence of osteoarthritis and the rising aging population, which is more susceptible to joint-related ailments. Additionally, advancements in injection techniques and a growing preference for minimally invasive procedures are contributing to market expansion.
