<p><p>The Polymer Cement Waterproof Coating market is segmented into two primary types. Type I, characterized by high elongation and a high poly-ash ratio, offers superior flexibility and durability, making it ideal for areas subject to movement and cracking. Type II, with low elongation and a low poly-ash ratio, provides a more rigid coating, suitable for less dynamic surfaces where less flexibility is required. Both types cater to diverse applications in construction, ensuring effective waterproofing based on specific project needs.</p></p>
