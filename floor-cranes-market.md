<p><p>The floor crane market is primarily divided into two main types: hydraulic floor cranes and electric floor cranes. Hydraulic floor cranes utilize a manual pump mechanism to lift and lower heavy loads, offering versatility and ease of use in various settings. In contrast, electric floor cranes are powered by electric motors, enabling more efficient lifting and operation, especially in high-demand environments. Both types cater to industries like manufacturing, warehousing, and construction, providing safe and effective material handling solutions.</p></p>
