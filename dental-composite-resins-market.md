<p><strong>The Dental Composite Resins Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Macrofilled Filler</li><li>Microfilled Filler</li><li>Hybrid Filler</li><li>Nanofilled Filler</li><li>Bulk Filler</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Dental composite resins are categorized based on filler particle sizes, impacting their properties and applications. Macrofilled fillers contain larger particles, offering strength but requiring more polishing. Microfilled fillers have smaller particles for improved aesthetics and polishability, ideal for anterior teeth. Hybrid fillers combine both sizes, providing a balance of strength and polishability for versatile use. Nanofilled fillers utilize nanotechnology, offering superior aesthetics and strength for fine details. Bulk-fill resins enable quicker application, allowing for deeper layers with reduced curing time.</p></p>
