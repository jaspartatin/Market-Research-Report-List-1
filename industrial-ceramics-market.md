<p><p>The industrial ceramics market comprises various types, including alumina, mullite, cordierite, and others. Alumina, known for its high strength and thermal resistance, is widely used in abrasives and refractory applications. Mullite exhibits excellent thermal stability, making it ideal for kiln furniture and insulators. Cordierite is valued for its low thermal expansion and thermal shock resistance, often used in ceramic substrates and refractory materials. Other ceramics encompass a range of specialty materials tailored for specific industrial applications, enhancing overall performance and durability.</p></p>
