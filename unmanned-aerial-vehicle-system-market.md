<p><p>The Unmanned Aerial Vehicle (UAV) System Market comprises several types. Fixed-wing UAVs are designed for long-distance flight and efficiency, ideal for surveillance and mapping. Rotary wing UAVs, including drones, offer vertical takeoff and hovering capabilities, suitable for tasks like search and rescue. Flapping-wing UAVs mimic bird flight for stealth and agility, suitable for close-range reconnaissance. Airship UAVs use lighter-than-air technology for persistent observation and monitoring, providing stable flight for extended periods over large areas.</p></p>
