<p><p>Prism Films operates in two primary market types: Single Layer Film and Multi Layer Film. Single Layer Film consists of a single polymer layer, offering simplicity and cost-effectiveness, making it suitable for basic packaging needs. In contrast, Multi Layer Film combines several layers of different materials, enhancing barrier properties, durability, and versatility. This advanced design caters to specialized applications such as food preservation and industrial uses, ensuring longer shelf life and improved performance in demanding environments.</p></p>
