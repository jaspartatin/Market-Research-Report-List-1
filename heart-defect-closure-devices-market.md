<p><strong>The Heart Defect Closure Devices Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>ASD Closure Devices</li><li>VSD Closure Devices</li><li>PDA Closure Devices</li><li>PFO Closure Devices</li><li>LAA Closure Devices</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Heart defect closure devices are medical instruments designed to repair specific congenital heart defects. ASD (Atrial Septal Defect) closure devices address openings between the heart's upper chambers, while VSD (Ventricular Septal Defect) devices target holes between the lower chambers. PDA (Patent Ductus Arteriosus) closure devices close blood flow between the aorta and pulmonary artery. PFO (Patent Foramen Ovale) closure devices seal openings in the heart that can lead to complications. LAA (Left Atrial Appendage) closure devices prevent blood clots in the left atrium, reducing stroke risk.</p></p>
