<p><p>The 5G small cell market includes three main types: micro, pico, and femto cells. Micro small cells cover larger areas, typically used in urban environments to enhance network capacity and coverage. Pico small cells serve smaller geographic areas, ideal for high-density locations like shopping malls and stadiums. Femto small cells are designed for home or small office use, providing localized coverage for a limited number of users. Together, these small cells enable efficient 5G deployment, enhancing connectivity and network performance in diverse settings.</p></p>
