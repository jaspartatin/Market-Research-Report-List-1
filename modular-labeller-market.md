<p><ul><li>Straight Line Labeling Machine</li><li>Rotary Labeling Machine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The modular labeller market consists of two main types: straight line labeling machines and rotary labeling machines. Straight line labeling machines operate by moving products in a linear path, applying labels as they advance through the system, ideal for lower-speed applications. In contrast, rotary labeling machines employ a circular motion, allowing for faster and more efficient labeling, suitable for high-speed production environments. Both types are crucial for various industries, ensuring accurate and effective labeling of products.</p></p>
