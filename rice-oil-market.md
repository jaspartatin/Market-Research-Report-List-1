<p><ul><li>Extraction</li><li>Squeezing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The rice oil market can be categorized into two primary types: extraction and squeezing. Extraction involves employing methods like solvent extraction or cold pressing to separate oil from rice bran, yielding a higher product volume. In contrast, the squeezing method, often referred to as pressing, uses mechanical pressure to extract oil, which can result in a more natural flavor and fewer additives. Each method impacts the quality, yield, and applications of rice oil, catering to diverse consumer preferences and market demands.</p></p>
